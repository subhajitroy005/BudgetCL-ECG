# Code of conduct

## Our standard

This is a scientific repository. Beyond ordinary respect, the standard is
**intellectual honesty**: report what the data show, including when it is
inconvenient.

Expected:

- disagree with claims, methods, and results directly and with evidence;
- report defects in the work, including in the published numbers;
- accept correction without treating it as a personal matter.

Not acceptable:

- personal attacks, harassment, or demeaning comments;
- overstating results, or pressuring others to;
- publishing others' private information.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer
directly. Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially where possible.

## Attribution

Adapted from the spirit of the Contributor Covenant, with a scientific-integrity
clause added deliberately: in a research artifact, misrepresenting results is a
conduct problem, not only a technical one.
